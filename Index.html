Before You Arrive

Farm Rules & Guidelines

To ensure a safe, respectful experience for all families, please follow our farm rules.

⚠️
At Your Own Risk

All visitors are here at their own risk. The Lamb Place is not responsible for any injury, accident, or loss during your visit.

🔇
No Loud Music

Please keep music at a considerate volume. Be mindful of families around you.

🚭
No Smoking

Strictly prohibited anywhere on the property. This is a family environment with children present.

🤝
Treat Others With Respect

Treat every person here the way you would like to be treated. Kindness makes Eid special.

🗑️
Clean Up After Yourself

No littering. Leave your area cleaner than you found it. Respect the land we all share.

🚫
No Nudity

Family-friendly, faith-centered event. Appropriate dress and conduct required at all times.

🏡
Respect Our Property — This Is Private Land

You are a guest on our private farm. Respect all buildings, fences, equipment, and animals. Do not enter restricted areas. This is not public property — treat it with the care you would want shown to your own home.

📋By visiting The Lamb Place you agree to follow these rules. Visitors who fail to comply may be asked to leave the property.
